h1(order-613). Finding more recent communities

p.  _To find the communities created on the system in recent times._

*  "How to access":#howaccess
*  "Description":#description
*  "Related topics":#relatedtopics

h2(#howaccess). How to access

p. Find on top of page the term "communities" and click on the arrow below it:
  !=/images/doc/menu-more-recent-communities.en.png(More recent communities on top menu)!

h2(#description). Description
# The communities will be listed ordered by creation date with most recent first. Below the profile's picture, you will see the date that the community was created !=/images/doc/more-recent-communities-detail.en.png(Profile with creation date)!
# Fill in the search field with what you want to look for and click on "Search" !=/images/doc/more-recent-communities.en.png(More recent communities search field)!
# Then you will see the search results ordered by creation date !=/images/doc/more-recent-communities-with-example.en.png(More recent communities search results with example)!
# Clicking on one of the search results, you will be redirected to the homepage of choosen community.
