h1(order-650). Finding enterprises

p.  _To find an enterprise in the system, you can use the search specific for enterprises._

*  "How to access":#howaccess
*  "Description":#description
*  "Related topics":#relatedtopics

h2(#howaccess). How to access

p. Find on top of page the term "Enterprises" and click on it:
  !=/images/doc/menu-enterprises.en.png(Enterprises on top menu)!

h2(#description). Description

# Fill in the search field with what you want to look for and click on "Search"  !=/images/doc/enterprises-search-field.en.png(Enterprises search field)!
# Then you will see the search results. To see more results, click on the pages below the search results.  !=/images/doc/enterprises-search-results-with-example.en.png(Enterprises search results with example)!
# Clicking on one of the search results, you will be redirected to the homepage of choosen enterprise.

h3. More options

p. In all kind of search you can specify the search clicking on "More options" link. The options displayed are explained in "Advanced search":/doc/navigation/advanced-search

h3. Display on map

p. If available on the system, when you are searching for products, services or enterprises and the system allows, you will see the "Display on map" button. Clicking on this button, the search results will be displayed on a map, with house icons indicating the location of enterprises.
