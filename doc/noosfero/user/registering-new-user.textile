h1(order-400). Registering a new user

p.  _To be part of the system, you need to register yourself. After registering, you will be able to join communities, enterprises, add friends and much more._

*  "How to access":#howaccess
*  "Description":#description
*  "Related Topics":#relatedtopics

h2(#howaccess). How to access

# In the system homepage you will see a box to get into the system.
# Click on "New user":  !=/images/doc/box-register.en.png(Register on box)!

h2(#description). Description

# You will see registration form. Fill it with you information and read and accept the system terms of use.
** The fields with red names are mandatory, so your registration won't be done if they are not filled in.
** Your e-mail and login must be unique within the system. So, if you fill in with a login/e-mail that already exists in the system, a message will be shown informing the error and you should choose another login/e-mail.
** After clicking the "Sign up" button you will be redirected to your control panel, where you can change your settings and start using the system.
