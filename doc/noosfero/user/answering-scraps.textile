h1(order-456). Answering scraps sent by friends

p.  _When a friend sends a scrap to you, you can answer it._

*  "How to access":#howaccess
*  "Description":#description
*  "Related Topics":#relatedtopics

h2(#howaccess). How to access

# Visit your profile to see the scraps that your friends sent to you. !=/images/doc/other-person-view-profile-link.en.png(Link to view profile)!

h2(#description). Description

# On you profile, when you click on "Wall" tab, the scraps sent to you will be listed. !=/images/doc/person-wall-scrap-sent.en.png(Wall tab)!
# When you hover the mouse on the scrap, click on the "Reply" button that will be displayed !=/images/doc/person-wall-scrap-reply-button.en.png(Wall tab)!
# To answer the scrap, fill in the field and press the "Leave a scrap" button !=/images/doc/person-wall-scrap-reply-form.en.png(Wall tab)!
# Then, your reply will be listed on your profile's wall !=/images/doc/person-wall-scrap-reply-sent.en.png(Wall tab)!
