h1(order-10). Sending e-mail to members

p.  _If you are an environment administrator, you can send e-mail to all members of your environment._

*  "How to access":#howaccess
*  "Description":#description

h2(#howaccess). How to access

# Find your user menu on top bar:  !=/images/doc/system-homepage-top-menu-admin.en.png(Top menu)!
# In user menu, click on Administration:  !=/images/doc/menu-admin.en.png(Control panel on menu)!
# Then, click on "Manage users" link:  !=/images/doc/admin-panel-manage-users.en.png(Manage users in admin panel)!

h2(#description). Description

# In "Manage users", click on "Send e-mail to users" link:  !=/images/doc/admin-panel-send-email.en.png(Send e-mail link on manage users)!
# Fill in the "Subject" field with the subject of the e-mail and the "Body" field with the content of the e-mail you want to send. Then, click on "Send" !=/images/doc/admin-send-email.en.png(Sending e-mail to environment members)!
